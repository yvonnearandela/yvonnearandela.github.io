    Dashboard: Cost of Living in the Philippines

    Project Description

    Cost of living refers to a measurement of the needed amount of money to sustain the basic expenses of living, such as the food, housing, transport, energy, healthcare, and taxes, in a specific country or city. It is often used to compare how costly it is to live in a city than to others. This dashboard illustrates the cost of living in major cities in the Philippines in comparison with New York City with the use of MicroStrategy. It would aid possible migrants in decision-making whether to stay or live in a certain city through the different visualizations present in the dashboard.
